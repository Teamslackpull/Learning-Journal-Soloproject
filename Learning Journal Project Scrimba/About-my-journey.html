Hey, my name is Nabil, and welcome to my story about how I fell in love with programming.

When I first learned that a friend had developed the new fitness app for our group, i was really intrigued. It wasn't just admiration; it was an inspiration, a call to action. I found myself wanted to do the same thing to start a similar journey, to delve into the world of coding and creation. I directly, turned to Google, typing in: "how to program." Instantly, a flood of suggestions came to my screen, overwhelming yet enticing.

Despite my limited understanding, I was drawn to the strange lines of code, reminiscent of the cascading green symbols from the iconic Matrix movies. Undeterred by my lack of expertise, I delved into online tutorials, stumbling upon a YouTube video that promised to teach me all the ins and outs of programming. Following its instructions, I hastily downloaded an IDE and attempted to replicate the steps laid out before me. Yet, despite my efforts, success wasn't on my side.

Determined to follow this newfound passion, I persisted in my goal for knowledge. This time, I specified my search, focusing on the creation of websites, unaware of the difference between frontend and backend development. It was then that I stumbled upon Scrimba, a beacon of light in my journey towards digital knowledge. Enrolling in the HTML and CSS modules, I was transported into a world of creativity and design. With each lesson, I felt my skills growing, like a canvas coming to life under the masterful strokes of a painter's brush. It was a revelation, a realization of my innate connection to the art of digital creation. Growing up in a household brimming with creativity, with parents who are both designers, I had always felt the pull of artistic expression. Yet, I lacked the technical skills to bring my visions to life. Now, armed with the tools of coding and design, I am determined to transform my ideas into beautiful digital creations. And so, my journey continues, fueled by passion and guided by curiosity. With each line of code I write, I inch closer to realizing my dreams, creating a contribution towards the amazing world of digital masterpieces where there is are no bounds and creativity knows no limits.

"How I Maintain Commitment in Learning"

"I find it difficult to describe exactly what keeps me motivated to keep learning because I don't really need to be motivated. I just really love programming. It's kind of like when a friend invites you to do something fun; you don't really have to make an effort, it's just something you're naturally drawn to. Motivation is a feeling, and feelings are not consistent. So, I don't rely on the idea that my choices are dependent on my motivation. Instead, it should become a habit or just a part of you."
